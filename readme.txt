Transform WordPress into a Powerful Podcast Publishing Platform. Quickly generate RSS feeds and manage episodes with ease.

== Description ==

**PodPoster** revolutionizes your WordPress website, turning it into a complete and intuitive platform for podcast publishing. If you're eager to share your voice, your stories, or your expertise through a podcast, PodPoster streamlines the entire process, handling the technical heavy lifting so you can focus on creating compelling audio content.

This plugin seamlessly integrates robust podcasting capabilities directly into your existing WordPress environment. Say goodbye to complex external services or cumbersome setups. With PodPoster, you gain:

* **Effortless RSS Feed Generation:** At the heart of every successful podcast is a valid RSS feed. PodPoster automatically generates an industry-standard feed for your episodes. This is crucial for submitting your podcast to all major directories, including Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Google Podcasts, and many more, ensuring your audience can easily find and subscribe to your show wherever they listen.
* **Simplified Episode Management:** Publishing new episodes is as straightforward as creating a new post in WordPress. Easily upload your audio files, add episode titles, detailed descriptions, show notes, and eye-catching cover art directly within your familiar WordPress dashboard. PodPoster is designed to make your podcast publishing workflow as smooth and efficient as possible.
* **Complete Content Control:** By hosting your podcast directly on your WordPress site, you maintain full ownership and control over your valuable audio files and all your show's data. Avoid the limitations, restrictions, or recurring fees often associated with third-party podcast hosting platforms.
* **Designed for Speed and Simplicity:** PodPoster is built with the user in mind. You don't need to be a technical expert or a seasoned developer to get your podcast online quickly. From initial installation to publishing your very first episode, the process is streamlined, intuitive, and remarkably fast.

Whether you're an experienced podcaster seeking a more integrated and cost-effective solution or a complete beginner ready to launch your first show, PodPoster provides the powerful features and exceptional ease of use you need to succeed. Get ready to transform your WordPress site into a dynamic podcasting hub and amplify your message to a global audience!

== Installation ==

1. Upload the `podposter` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Go to the "PodPoster" settings panel in the admin dashboard to configure your podcast options.
4. Start adding podcast episodes using the new custom post type.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I submit my PodPoster RSS feed to Apple Podcasts (iTunes), Spotify, and Google Podcasts? =
Absolutely! The RSS feed generated by PodPoster is fully compliant with industry standards, making it ready for submission to all major podcast directories.

= Where are my RSS feeds located? =
Your feeds will be located in a `/rss/` folder inside your WordPress uploads directory. This is automatically created on activation.

= Does PodPoster display episodes on the front-end of my website? =
PodPoster primarily focuses on the **backend management and RSS feed generation** for your podcasts. It creates custom post types for your podcast channels and episodes. **By default, most WordPress themes do not automatically display custom post types in their standard loops (like blog posts).
